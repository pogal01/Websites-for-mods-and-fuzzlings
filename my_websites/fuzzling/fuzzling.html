The fuzzlings

These are the main type of Fuzzling they all originated from this spcific Fuzzling species.

The origin of the Fuzzlings.

Fuzzlings are creatures from the planet fuzz they, crashed on earth because there tiny planet got sucked into the gravitatinal field.

Now they are trying to fight for survival,during there fight for survivle some have evolved for certain reasons.

However, some Fuzzlings have become corrupt it is currently unknown how this has happend but be carefull

These corupt fuzzlings can be noticed by their steriotypical evil eyebrows and there fuzz balls are now devil horns.

They have also been nicknamed the corruptlings due to how they seem to be corrupt fuzzlings.

The sub species of Fuzzlings

Fuzzling with gray/silver skin + spikes = a spikeling.

climeling

Fuzzling with darker green skin than the hedgeling & moss and algie intead of fur = a swampling
